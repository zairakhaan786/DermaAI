What are Skin Diseases?

Skin diseases are conditions that affect your skin — causing rashes, inflammation, itchiness, or other changes. Some are genetic, while others arise from environmental or lifestyle factors. Early detection is critical for effective treatment.

DermaAI leverages a deep learning model trained on thousands of dermatological images to classify conditions like Melanoma, Atopic Dermatitis, and more — all in seconds.

Skin Conditions We Detect

Actinic Keratosis

Precancerous rough, scaly patches caused by prolonged sun exposure.

Atopic Dermatitis

Chronic, itchy condition causing redness, dryness, and inflammation.

Benign Keratosis

Non-cancerous skin growth, often rough, brown, or wart-like.

Dermatofibroma

Firm, small, harmless skin nodule, usually on legs or arms.

Melanocytic Nevus

Common moles; pigmented, benign growths from melanocytes.

Melanoma

Serious skin cancer from moles; may be dark, irregular, and fast-growing.

Squamous Cell Carcinoma

Common skin cancer; scaly red patches or sores that may bleed.
