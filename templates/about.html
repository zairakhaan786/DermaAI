About DermaAI

What is DermaAI?

DermaAI is an AI-powered medical web application designed to detect and classify skin diseases from images. This project was developed as an individual academic AI research project at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun, India.

Built using deep learning (ResNet50 architecture trained on the HAM10000 dataset), it can identify 9 types of skin conditions with high accuracy, delivering severity ratings and personalised skincare recommendations.

Try a Scan

How It Works

1. Upload Image

Take or upload a clear photo of the affected skin area. JPG, PNG, WebP, and BMP formats are all supported.

2. AI Analysis

A ResNet50-based deep learning model analyses the image and generates a prediction across 9 skin disease categories.

3. Get Results

View the predicted condition with a confidence score. Then consult a dermatologist for a professional diagnosis.
